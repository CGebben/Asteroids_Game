CONTROLS:

↑  Up Arrow     - Move Forward  
↓  Down Arrow   - Brake / Move Backward  
←  Left Arrow   - Rotate Left  
→  Right Arrow  - Rotate Right  
Spacebar        - Fire Bullet  
A Key           - Hyperspace Jump (teleport to a safe location)

GAME MECHANICS:

- Shooting larger asteroids causes them to split into smaller ones.
- Hyperspace Jump teleports you to a safe location.
- You are invincible for a few seconds:
  • at the start of the game  
  • after using hyperspace  
  • after losing a life
- You have 3 lives.
- Colliding with an asteroid costs 1 life.
- The game ends when all lives are lost or you complete all levels.
